01

Udyam Registration FREE

Your legal MSME identity. One Aadhaar + PAN turns your freelancing into a recognised business — the gateway that unlocks the other two portals.

udyamregistration.gov.in

✅ Who's eligible

  • Any micro / small / medium enterprise in services, manufacturing or trading — within investment & turnover limits (Micro = up to ₹2.5 cr investment / ₹10 cr turnover under 2025 rules).
  • Solo freelancers as proprietors qualify: designers, editors, coders, marketers, consultants.
  • You only need an Aadhaar + PAN. No document uploads — it runs on self-declaration.

🪜 Step by step

  1. Open the portal and choose "For New Entrepreneurs not registered as MSME".
  2. Enter your Aadhaar number + name, verify with OTP.
  3. Validate your PAN (tax-linked check happens automatically).
  4. Fill business details — activity, address, bank account — by self-declaration.
  5. Final submit + OTP. You instantly get a 19-character Udyam Number and e-certificate by email.

🎁 Why it's worth it

  • Permanent govt business identity with QR-verified certificate.
  • Delayed-payment protection — buyers must pay within 45 days or owe you interest (MSME Samadhaan).
  • Collateral-free loan eligibility (CGTMSE) and 50% subsidy on patent/trademark fees.
  • Access to MSME-reserved government tenders — and it's required to use GeM benefits & TReDS.
02

GeM Seller Portal

Government e-Marketplace — sell your services directly to central & state departments, PSUs and autonomous bodies. No middlemen, no broker.

gem.gov.in

✅ Who's eligible

  • Any seller or service provider — MSMEs, startups, manufacturers, and individual service providers / freelancers with genuine credentials.
  • Section 8 companies, registered trusts and societies can sell too.
  • Keep ready: PAN, Aadhaar-linked mobile, GSTIN (where applicable), bank details, and a business doc (your Udyam certificate works).

🪜 Step by step

  1. On gem.gov.in click Sign Up → Seller / Service Provider.
  2. Confirm the listed pre-requisites and documents.
  3. Enter Aadhaar / PAN + linked mobile + email, verify via OTP.
  4. Fill business details — legal name, type, address, GSTIN, bank, and the service categories you offer.
  5. Create your user ID & password, then complete bank verification.
  6. Finish your profile to 100% (note the Caution Money rule in 2026) and list your catalogue. Verification typically takes 15–20 days.

🎁 Why it's worth it

  • Direct access to a massive government buyer base — no agents in between.
  • Transparent bidding & reverse auctions; integrated online payments.
  • Special preference for Udyam / MSME-registered sellers.
  • Orders up to ₹25,000 can be placed with you directly, without a bid.
03

TReDS · Fast Payments

Trade Receivables Discounting System — an RBI-regulated platform where banks pay your approved invoice in days instead of you waiting 60–90 for the client.

treds.in  ·  RXIL / M1xchange / Invoicemart

✅ Who's eligible

  • A registered MSME (your Udyam number) supplying to large corporate buyers, PSUs or government departments.
  • Both you and your buyer register on a TReDS platform with KYC.
  • Best suited once you raise real invoices to bigger buyers — not tiny one-off gigs with individual clients.

🪜 Step by step

  1. Register on a TReDS platform (RXIL, M1xchange or Invoicemart) and complete KYC.
  2. Your buyer onboards on the same platform.
  3. Upload your approved invoice as a "Factoring Unit".
  4. Buyer approves it digitally, confirming the amount.
  5. Registered banks & NBFCs bid competitively to fund it.
  6. Accept the best bid — money lands in your account, often within 24–48 hours.
  7. On the due date, your buyer pays the financier directly.

🎁 Why it's worth it

  • Cash in days, not months — no more chasing 60–90 day payment cycles.
  • Competitive rates driven down by bank/NBFC bidding.
  • "Without recourse" — if your buyer defaults, the liability isn't yours.
  • Big buyers (turnover above ₹250 cr) & CPSEs are now mandated onto TReDS, so onboarding your client is easier.
💡 The smart order: Get Udyam first (it's your ID card), use it to register on GeM for government clients, then plug invoices into TReDS so you never wait months to get paid.
